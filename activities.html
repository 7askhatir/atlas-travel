FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking activities.

Booking is easy! Just click "Book Now" on any activity, or send us a message on WhatsApp. We'll confirm availability and send you all the details within minutes. Pay upon arrival or online — your choice.

We recommend comfortable clothing, sunscreen, a hat, and water. For desert activities, bring a light jacket for the evening. For surf lessons, we provide wetsuits and boards. A full packing list is sent with every booking confirmation.

Many of our activities are family-friendly! Camel rides, desert safaris, and the hot air balloon are suitable for children. Quad biking requires minimum age 12. Check the age rating on each activity card or ask us for recommendations.

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries. Many guests combine a morning camel ride with an afternoon quad biking session, or a desert safari followed by an overnight camp. Message us and we'll build your perfect day.

Most activities include pickup and drop-off from your accommodation. For desert activities, we include transport from Marrakech. If you need additional transport, check our private transfer options on the Transport page.

Your safety comes first. In case of extreme weather, we'll reschedule your activity at no cost or provide a full refund. We monitor conditions closely and communicate any changes at least 2 hours before your booking.
