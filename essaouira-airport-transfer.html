Airport Information

About Essaouira Mogador Airport (ESU)

Everything you need to know about Essaouira Mogador Airport.

Airport Code: ESU

Essaouira Mogador Airport (IATA: ESU, ICAO: GMMI) is a small international airport located approximately 15 km south of Essaouira city centre. It serves domestic and seasonal international flights, primarily from Europe.

Location & Access

The airport is located on the N1 highway, about a 15-minute drive from the centre of Essaouira. Taxis are available outside arrivals, but pre-booking ensures a fixed price and guaranteed availability.

Airport Facilities

ESU offers a café, car rental desks, ATM, and a small duty-free shop. The terminal is compact and easy to navigate. Free WiFi is available throughout the terminal building.

Popular Destinations from ESU

Direct flights connect Essaouira to Paris, London, Brussels, and other European cities. Seasonal routes include Ryanair from London Stansted, Transavia from Paris Orly, and TUI fly from Brussels.

Transfer Time to City Centre

The drive from Essaouira Airport to the city centre takes approximately 15 minutes. To the Essaouira medina it takes around 20 minutes, and to Tagharte beach about 25 minutes.

Why Pre-Book a Transfer?

Pre-booking guarantees a fixed price, flight tracking, meet & greet service, and avoids haggling with local taxis. Your driver waits for you even if your flight is delayed — completely free.
