Essaouira Mogador Airport (IATA: ESU, ICAO: GMMI) is a modern regional airport located approximately 16 kilometers northeast of Essaouira city center. Opened in 2010, the airport serves as the main gateway for travelers visiting Essaouira and the surrounding region. It handles both domestic and seasonal international flights from European cities such as Paris, Brussels, London, and Madrid, operated by airlines including Ryanair, Transavia, TUI Fly, and Royal Air Maroc.
